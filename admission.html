Admission Requirements
  • Educational Background:

    • Applicants should provide evidence that they successfully completed a 13-year education system from their home country or the country where they studied.

    Applicants without the above will be required to our Certificate of Information Technology to be eligible for Diploma programs

  • Language Proficiency:

    • All courses are conducted in English, so applicants must have a basic understanding of the English language to effectively engage with the curriculum and participate in discussions.

  • Age and Origin

    • Be 17 -34 years old and of African Origin.
